Frequently Asked Questions




LevelUp is a platform that you use to recognize each other in your team or across the organization for appreciating their work. LevelUp aids in enhancing and managing individual and team’s performances. .

Across an organization, LevelUp is used by an employee and employer to manage their growth. At the same time, at an individual level, it sets the individual to plan and achieve growth at break-neck speeds purely by performance and contribution. .

Targets and KPIs are related to either an individual or a team and is set by the respective Manager. This option is not available for an individual when she logs into LevelUp.

Targets are aligned with Organizational Goals and can be daily, weekly, monthly quarterly, half-yearly or annual targets.

As motivation and recognition are essential, Core Values and Kudos can be given and received by any individual at any level across the organization.

There is no limit for giving kudos to an individual. The idea is to appreciate good work and recognizing the same – is there an end to doing good work?

While only 6 core values can be given to or by an individual, this number gets refreshed at the beginning of every quarter. There is no carry-forward allowed. Either the core values or given within the quarter or they get exhausted. .



As motivation and recognition are essential, Core Values and Kudos can be given and received by any individual at any level across the organization. .

Each core value has a weightage of 2 points. Every quarter, a user can give only 6 core values to others across the teams

Under Give Recognition page, under Kudos Received tab the user can see the list of Kudos that the user has received from others across the organization.

Under Give Recognition page, The Top Giver tab represents the list of employees from who I have received the Core Value stickers (these add up to the points shown on the same page at the rate of 2 points for each Core Value).

Under Give Recognition page, The Top Receiver tab represents the list of employees to who I have given the Core Value stickers.

Under Give Recognition page, under Kudos Given tab the user can see the list of Kudos that the user has given to others across the organization.
